About

Anatomical Consulting LLC is led by Dr. Derek Harmon, a PhD anatomist, Associate Professor - Clinical, and Director of Continuing Education for the Division of Anatomy at The Ohio State University College of Medicine. Previously, Dr. Harmon served as the Director of Anatomy at the University of California, San Francisco School of Medicine. He has over 10 years of gross anatomy teaching experience for learners at every level: medical students, residents, fellows, and practicing clinicians.

He has developed, organized, and coordinated numerous workshops and continuing education courses, both internally and externally alongside surgical colleagues, serving as both teacher and course coordinator. Whether you are launching a CE course, redesigning a curriculum, or evaluating educational technology, Dr. Harmon brings the expertise to keep your project on track from start to finish.
